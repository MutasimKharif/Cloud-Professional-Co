Our Story

Founded in Riyadh by a team of passionate, AWS-certified engineers, CloudPros was born from a simple observation: there was a critical gap between the immense potential of cloud technology and its adoption by businesses in the Middle East. We set out not just to offer services, but to become true partners in digital transformation.

From our first successful migration to becoming a trusted name in cloud strategy, DevOps, and security, our journey has been defined by a relentless commitment to our clients' success. We believe in empowering regional businesses with the world-class tools and expertise they need to compete on a global scale.
