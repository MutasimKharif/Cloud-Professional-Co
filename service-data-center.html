Our Comprehensive Data Center Services

A modern data center is the heart of your IT operations. CloudPros provides a full spectrum of services to ensure your physical infrastructure is robust, resilient, and ready to meet the demands of your business. We handle every aspect of the data center lifecycle.

IT Data Center Build

We manage greenfield data center projects from the ground up, including site selection, architectural design, and construction, ensuring the final facility meets industry best practices and your specific operational requirements.

VPC Peering

Protect your critical hardware with a state-of-the-art EMS. We deploy sensors to monitor temperature, humidity, airflow, water leaks, and power usage, providing real-time alerts to prevent environmental threats from causing downtime.

Fire Fighting System

Safety is paramount. We install advanced fire suppression systems, such as clean agent (FM-200, Novec 1230) solutions, that extinguish fires quickly without damaging sensitive electronic equipment, ensuring business continuity.

Air Precision Cooling System

High-density computing requires specialized cooling. We design and install Computer Room Air Conditioning (CRAC) and Air Handling (CRAH) units to maintain precise temperature and humidity levels, guaranteeing optimal performance and longevity of your servers.
